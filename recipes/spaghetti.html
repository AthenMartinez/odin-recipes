Spaghetti

Description

This recipe has been handed down through a generation. It is striking in both the its flavor and simplicity. Pairs well with orange juice and bar of chocolate.

For whatever reason, I am the only one in this house capable of making something of this magnitude. I hope my wife never sees this page...

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Ground Beef or Turkey
  • Love

Steps

  1. Boil water lovingly
  2. Brown the meat in a pan (also lovingly)
  3. Once the meat is browned, add the tomato sauce
  4. Once the water is boiling, add noodles
  5. Add Italian Seasoning, Garlic Powder, and Onion Powder to the meat and sacue to taste
  6. Once the noodles are done, drain water
  7. Enjoy the secret family recipe
