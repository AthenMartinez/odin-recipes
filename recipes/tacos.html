White People Tacos

Description

Like many recipes on this website, these tacos have been passed down through a generation. Not many white people can make them with the half mexican claim that I have. (satire)

Love them or hate them these will get the job done. Best paired with orange juice and a bar of chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Taco Tortillas
  • Ground Meat
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Mexican Cheese
  • Yellow Rice
  • Refried Beans (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil Water for the rice
  2. Brown the meat in a pan
  3. Once meat is brown add a little water and taco seasoning
  4. Once water is boiling, add contents of the rice box
  5. Once rice and meat are finished, make beans in microwave
  6. Assemble on tortillas and add hot sauce
